Head trainers

Leading the performance floor

Angus

Co-Founder · Head Trainer

Angus brings over 16 years of experience in golf-specific training. He fell for the game at six, watching his father practice on the range, and competed in golf and basketball through high school — where injuries and a lack of strength showed him how much the body drives the swing. That sparked a passion for strength and conditioning at UC Irvine, where he interned as a physical therapy aide.

Today he builds custom programs blending functional movement, body-swing analysis, and golf-specific drills to develop power, speed, balance, and injury resilience — known for delivering measurable results that help players of every level play stronger, smarter, and longer.

Elliot Bunyaviroch

Co-Founder · Head Trainer

A four-year collegiate golfer and former Captain of the UC San Diego men's golf team, Elliot brings a player's-eye perspective to performance coaching. He co-founded The Turn in Dublin, California, after years spent in golf instruction and community development — from coaching juniors at Lake Merced Golf Club to building programs that bring players together.

He works with members across every level, from first-timers to competitive amateurs, pairing technical instruction with the team-first mindset he led with in college to help players build a more confident, repeatable game.
